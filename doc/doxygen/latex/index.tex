\begin{DoxyItemize}
\item {\bfseries{Intra-\/vehicle}} messages are sent by the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_agent}{Agent}} to the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_c_a_n}{CAN} BUS} and routed to the other {\ttfamily Agents}.
\item {\bfseries{Inter-\/vehicle}} messages are sent by the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_agent}{Agent}} to the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_c_a_n}{CAN} BUS}, routed to the {\ttfamily \doxylink{class_gateway}{Gateway}} where they go down the stack and are broadcasted.
\item Dual Observer patterns decouple layers and provide thread-\/safe delivery.
